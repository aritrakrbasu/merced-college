About the Department

Department of Commerce is dedicated to blend technical excellence with strong fundamental skills and values. The teaching methodology of the department is to provide our students the real education which is not based on factual knowledge, but reality of life based on values and ethics. This discipline affects all other disciplines and enlightens the student through Jeevan mantra.

Infrastructure

  • Broadband NET and Wi-Fi facility.
  • Classroom with LCD Projector and lecture theatre to accommodate large number of undergraduate and post-graduate students.
  • Well equipped computer lab with facility of accounting & others related software.
  • Well equipped seminar hall
  • Central and Departmental library consisting of huge no. of books, dissertations, journals and others downloaded e-journals and e-books to facilitate our students.
